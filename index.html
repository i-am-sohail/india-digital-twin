💡 The problem we're solving

Tamil Nadu's government publishes an enormous amount of real data — census figures, weather records, air quality readings, water and energy dashboards — but it's scattered across dozens of separate websites. A district collector, a student, or an ordinary citizen has no single place to see the full picture of a district and get a clear, plain-language answer to "what should we prioritize here?"

Our solution: a "digital twin" — one live dashboard per district that pulls together real government and open data (never simulated numbers), and uses the Claude AI model as a decision-support layer that reads that real data and explains what it means in plain language, so anyone can ask it a question and get a grounded, data-backed answer.
